Overview

Since I was born, I have always had a natural gift for rythym and drumming. I have never had the home space or money to afford my own drumset, but luckily I had friends who let me play on theirs while being in a band. I have always wanted to have my very own custom drumset made by Truth. So I have here a picture of what I would idealy have as my set and a list of the pieces I would need to make this set.


List of Pieces Needed:

  • Bass drum
  • Double kick pedal
  • Snare drum
  • One high tom
  • Floor tom
  • High hat
  • Right and left crash cymbals
  • One ride cymbal
  • One china cymbal
  • One splash cymbal
